Our Mission

Hello, My name is Paul and I am a Marine Corps combat veteran who served three tours in Iraq. I've walked through the fire - PTSD, Depression, Anxiety, Nightmares, Anger Issues, Job losses, Addiction, and Homelessness.

This hub was created because St. Louis has incredible veteran services, but they're scattered and hard to find when you're in crisis. This is a centralized hub for every veteran resource in the St. Louis region (50-mile radius).

Whether you're:

  • A combat veteran struggling with PTSD
  • Experiencing homelessness or housing instability
  • Looking for employment or career training
  • Battling addiction or mental health issues
  • Needing benefits assistance
  • Supporting a veteran family member

You'll find the help you need here. All resources are free or low-cost for veterans.

Warrior to Warrior Recovery Manual

A comprehensive guide for combat veterans struggling with PTSD, depression, anxiety, anger, and the invisible wounds of war.

Written by Paul Moore, USMC combat veteran, based on personal experience and years of recovery work. This manual covers:

  • Understanding PTSD, depression, and anxiety
  • Daily battle rhythm for mental health
  • Managing anger and preventing explosions
  • Rebuilding relationships with family
  • Finding purpose after service
  • Career guidance for veterans
  • Crisis management and suicide prevention
  • 30-day action plan and long-term recovery roadmap
